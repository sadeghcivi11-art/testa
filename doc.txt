These creatures patrol in a straight line at a constant speed, ignoring the player's position. If a basic ground enemy encounters an obstacle like a wall or reaches the edge of a cliff, it will turn around and continue patrolling in the opposite direction. After patrolling in a straight path for a set period, the enemy stops and enters a brief resting idle state. Husk Hornhead Creature: This creature acts as a more advanced version of the basic ground enemies, though its fundamental movement mechanics remain similar.
